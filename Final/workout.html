Beginner Full Body Workout

This workout is perfect for beginners and targets all major muscle groups.

Exercises:

  • Squats - 3 sets of 12 reps

HIIT Cardio Workout

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) is a great way to burn fat and improve cardiovascular fitness.

Exercises:

  • Jumping Jacks - 30 seconds
  • Burpees - 30 seconds
  • Mountain Climbers - 30 seconds
  • Rest - 30 seconds
  • Repeat 4 times
