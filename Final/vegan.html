Vegan Diet Plans

Whole Food Vegan Diet

The whole food vegan diet is based on whole, unprocessed plant foods. It includes a variety of vegetables, fruits, legumes, nuts, seeds, and whole grains.

Foods:

  • Vegetables (spinach, kale, broccoli)
  • Fruits (apples, bananas, berries)
  • Legumes (lentils, chickpeas, black beans)
  • Nuts and seeds (almonds, chia seeds, flaxseeds)
  • Whole grains (quinoa, brown rice, oats)
  • Tofu and tempeh
  • Plant-based milks (almond milk, soy milk)
  • Healthy oils (olive oil, coconut oil)

Raw Vegan Diet

The raw vegan diet consists of raw or minimally heated foods. It emphasizes fresh fruits and vegetables, nuts, seeds, and sprouted grains and legumes.

Foods:

  • Fresh fruits (mangoes, pineapples, oranges)
  • Fresh vegetables (carrots, bell peppers, cucumbers)
  • Raw nuts and seeds (walnuts, sunflower seeds, hemp seeds)
  • Sprouted grains (sprouted quinoa, sprouted buckwheat)
  • Sprouted legumes (sprouted lentils, sprouted chickpeas)
  • Cold-pressed oils (olive oil, flaxseed oil)
  • Sea vegetables (nori, kelp, dulse)

High Carb Low Fat Vegan Diet

This diet emphasizes high-carb, low-fat plant foods. It includes a large amount of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and legumes, while minimizing fats.

Foods:

  • Fruits (bananas, mangoes, dates)
  • Starchy vegetables (potatoes, sweet potatoes, corn)
  • Whole grains (brown rice, oats, barley)
  • Legumes (black beans, lentils, peas)
  • Leafy greens (spinach, kale, lettuce)
  • Limited nuts and seeds
  • Limited healthy fats (avocado)

Vegan Mediterranean Diet

This diet combines the principles of vegan eating with the Mediterranean diet. It includes a variety of plant-based foods rich in healthy fats, proteins, and complex carbohydrates.

Foods:

  • Vegetables (tomatoes, cucumbers, artichokes)
  • Fruits (figs, grapes, pomegranates)
  • Legumes (lentils, chickpeas, white beans)
  • Whole grains (farro, bulgur, quinoa)
  • Nuts and seeds (almonds, pistachios, sesame seeds)
  • Olive oil
  • Herbs and spices (basil, oregano, rosemary)
  • Plant-based proteins (tofu, tempeh)
