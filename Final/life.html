Lifestyle Diseases

Cardiovascular Disease

Commonly linked to poor diet, lack of exercise, smoking, and excessive alcohol consumption. Preventive measures include a balanced diet, regular physical activity, avoiding tobacco, and managing stress effectively.

Stroke

Often caused by high blood pressure, smoking, obesity, and physical inactivity. Reducing salt intake, quitting smoking, maintaining a healthy weight, and regular exercise are essential for prevention and reducing risk.

More
Diabetes

Associated with obesity, poor diet, and inactivity. Prevention includes maintaining a healthy weight, eating a balanced diet, regular physical activity, and monitoring blood sugar levels to manage risk effectively.

More
Chronic Respiratory Diseases

Includes COPD and asthma, often exacerbated by smoking, air pollution, and occupational hazards. Quitting smoking, avoiding pollutants, and using protective gear can help prevent these conditions.

More
Cancer

Various types influenced by smoking, poor diet, excessive alcohol consumption, and lack of exercise. Prevention includes avoiding tobacco, eating a healthy diet, limiting alcohol, and regular screenings for early detection.

More
Obesity

Linked to poor diet and lack of physical activity. Maintaining a balanced diet, regular exercise, and a healthy lifestyle can help prevent obesity and related health problems.

More
Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Often related to high salt intake, lack of exercise, stress, and poor diet. Managing stress, reducing salt intake, regular physical activity, and a healthy diet are key preventive measures.

More
Liver Disease

Conditions like cirrhosis and liver cancer are influenced by excessive alcohol consumption, poor diet, and viral infections. Preventive measures include limiting alcohol, healthy diet, and vaccination against hepatitis.

More
Osteoporosis

Linked to poor diet (low calcium and vitamin D), lack of exercise, smoking, and excessive alcohol. Prevention includes a diet rich in calcium and vitamin D, regular weight-bearing exercise, and avoiding smoking.

More
Mental Health Disorders

Conditions such as depression and anxiety are influenced by stress, lack of sleep, poor diet, and inactivity. Preventive measures include managing stress, regular physical activity, healthy diet, and adequate sleep.
